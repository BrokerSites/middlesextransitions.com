Elegibility

To ensure the best fit and success for everyone at Middlesex Transitions, we kindly ask that potential residents meet the following criteria for admission:

  • Be at least 18 years old.
  • Complete resident application (DOWNLOAD APPLICATION)
  • Display a genuine motivation for recovery, openness to therapy, and the ability to seek and maintain employment if possible as part of their personal recovery journey.

Please note that we understand everyone's journey is unique, and we consider individual circumstances with compassion and care.
