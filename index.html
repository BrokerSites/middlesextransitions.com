Welcome!

Welcome to Middlesex Transitions!
Since 2007, we've been dedicated to fostering a supportive and high-quality sober living community in the Greater Boston area. Guided by a belief that recovery is a continuous journey, we offer a safe, nurturing environment for men overcoming alcoholism and drug addiction.

Our certified residences emphasize personal growth, mutual respect, and a commitment to a substance-free life. Dive deeper to discover our mission, values, and how we prioritize each individual's unique path to recovery.
